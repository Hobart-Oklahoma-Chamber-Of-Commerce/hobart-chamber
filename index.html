About the Chamber

Building a Vibrant Future for Hobart

The Hobart Chamber of Commerce is committed to supporting and promoting the businesses that make our community strong. Our mission is to encourage economic growth by connecting local businesses, attracting new opportunities, and creating a thriving environment for entrepreneurs and citizens alike.

We believe a strong business community leads to a strong Hobart. Through year-round events for adults, children, and families, we bring people together, drive traffic to local businesses, and showcase everything our town has to offer.
